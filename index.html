About Me

Hello! My name is Aldreck, and I’m an IT student specializing in Animation and Game Development. I consider myself a jack of all trades currently exploring which area of the game industry I want to master. When developing games, I take on creative and technical roles that let me use my strengths in game design, lore crafting, and music composition, while also applying my skills in programming and art. I’m passionate about building worlds, telling stories, and bringing ideas to life.
